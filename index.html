About us

We’re Dragontree Digital Media, a film and video production company devoted to producing exceptional creative, commercial and corporate content nationally.

We specialise in corporate content, and marketing and promotional materials as well as music videos, and special events such as weddings. With our end-to-end service, we can help you every step of the way – from concepts, planning and storyboarding to scriptwriting, voiceovers, filming and editing.

When you work with us, you’ll be assigned a dedicated team of friendly professionals. They work hard to get to know you and understand your requirements. We welcome you to see us as an extension of your own team.

So, whether you’re looking for a quick quote for a personal event or want to discuss a brief for your business, Gareth, our creative director, would love to hear from you.
